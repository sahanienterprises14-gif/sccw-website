About Us

Sahani Civil Construction is a trusted name in civil, structural, waterproofing, renovation, and turnkey interior projects. Established in 1986, we bring quality craftsmanship and engineering excellence to every project we undertake.

Our Core Services

RCC & Structural Works

Footings, columns, beams, slabs, staircases, shuttering, and reinforcement.

Waterproofing

Terrace, toilet, tank waterproofing, coating, grouting & protection systems.

Electrical Works

Wiring, boards, earthing, lighting, panels, CCTV & fire systems.

Plumbing & Sanitary

Water supply, drainage, sanitary fittings, tanks, pumps, RWH systems.

Wood & Carpentry

Furniture, doors, windows, modular cabinets, and finishing works.

False Ceiling & Interiors

POP, gypsum, grid ceilings, partitions, wall finishes, lighting.

Flooring & Tiling

Tile, marble, wooden flooring with professional installation.

Fabrication & Welding

Structural steel, railing, gates, staircases, on-site welding.
